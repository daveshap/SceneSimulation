Wang finishes setting up his stall just as the first customers begin to trickle in. He greets them warmly, and they make their way over to his stall. They begin to peruse the seafood, asking Wang about various types of fish. He is happy to answer their questions, and soon they are making their selections. Wang expertly cleans and prepares the fish, wrapping them up in newspaper. The customers are happy with their purchase, and they head off down the beach. Wang is glad to have made some early sales, and he is looking forward to a busy day. He knows that the key to a successful business is to always be prepared and to give the customers what they want.
