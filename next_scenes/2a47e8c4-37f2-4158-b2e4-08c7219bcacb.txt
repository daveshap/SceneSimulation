Wang drives away from the hotel, his mind heavy with the woman's story. He can't help but feel guilty that he was able to drive away and leave her there, while she was homeless and alone. Wang decides to drive around the city for a while, to clear his head. As he drives, he notices a group of people standing on the side of the road. They look like they are about to start a protest or something. Wang slows down to take a closer look. Suddenly, the group of people start throwing rocks and bricks at his truck! Wang ducks down in his seat to avoid being hit, and quickly drives away. He can hear the sound of the rocks hitting his truck as he drives away. Wang is shaken by the incident, but he continues driving. He knows that he must be careful from now on.
