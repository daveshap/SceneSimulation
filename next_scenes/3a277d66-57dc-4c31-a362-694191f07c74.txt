Jenna's heart races as she backs away slowly from the jaguar stalking her. She knows she's not going to make it out of the forest alive. The jaguar springs forward, knocking Jenna to the ground. Jenna screams as the jaguar sinks its teeth into her neck. She feels the life draining out of her as she struggles to free herself. The jaguar finally releases her and she lies still on the ground, her lifeblood stains the leaves around her.
