Swathi is sitting in her office, thinking about the close call she had with the avalanche. She is grateful to be alive, but the experience was very traumatizing. She can't stop thinking about the few moments when she thought she was going to die. She is trying to distract herself by working, but she can't focus. Suddenly, she hears a loud noise. It sounds like an avalanche! Swathi looks around in panic and sees a huge wall of snow coming towards her. She has no time to run. She can only watch as the avalanche engulfs her. Fortunately, Swathi is buried alive. She is able to reach her phone and call for help. After what feels like hours, she is finally rescued. She is cold and shaken, but otherwise unharmed. This was a close call for Swathi. She will never forget the experience of being buried alive.
