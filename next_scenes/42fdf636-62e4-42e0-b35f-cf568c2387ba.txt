The next day, Wang is at work when he gets a call from Mrs. Chen. She tells him that her daughter is missing again and she is frantic. She asks him if he can help her look for her. Wang agrees and he finishes his work for the day. He then meets Mrs. Chen at the beach and they begin their search. It is getting dark and Wang is about to give up when he hears a sound coming from the bushes. He cautiously approaches and sees the little girl hiding there. Mrs. Chen is overjoyed to see her daughter safe and Wang is relieved that he was able to help. They both go home, tired but satisfied.
