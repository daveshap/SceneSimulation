She went to the jewelry store to find her husband. He was standing in the window, looking at the necklace she had been eyeing for weeks. She walked up to him and put her hand on his shoulder. "I'm sorry for everything," she said. "I hope you can find it in your heart to forgive me." He turned to look at her, and she could see the pain in his eyes. "I forgive you," he said. "I love you." She put her arms around him and they embraced.
